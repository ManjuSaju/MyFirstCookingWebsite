      Butter cake  

Ingredients
250g  Unsalted Butter, softened
1 cup caster sugar
2 tsp vanilla essence
3 eggs
2 1/2 cups self-raising flour
2/3 cup milk
Soft Icing Mixture


     
 Preheat oven to 180°C/160°C fan-forced. 
 Grease and line an 8cm-deep,19cm (base) square cake pan with baking powder.
 Using an electric mixer, cream butter,caster sugar and vanilla in a medium bowl on medium-high speed until light and fluffy.
 Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating to combine. 
 Add half the flour. Stir to combine. 
 Add half the milk. Stir to combine. Repeat with remaining flour and milk.
 Spread mixture into prepared pan. Bake for 1 hour or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. 
 Stand in pan for 10 minutes. Turn out onto a wire rack to cool
